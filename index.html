About Chase Atlantic

Chase Atlantic is an Australian alternative R&B band formed in 2011. The group consists of Mitchel Cave, Christian Anthony, and Clinton Cave. Known for blending R&B, rock, and pop influences, Chase Atlantic has built a dedicated fanbase around the world.

Discography

Notable Albums

Chase Atlantic has released several albums and EPs, including their self-titled debut album "Chase Atlantic" (2017), "Phases" (2019), and "BEAUTY IN DEATH" (2021). Each album showcases their evolving sound and artistic growth.
